Where Im From

As you know, we are all from the planet Htrae, a land of crazy powers and wild monsters. Our wonderful, cylinder shaped world is home to all sorts of strange phenomena, like aliens, monsters, and all sorts of wild and wacky superpowers. Of course, when there are all sorts of powers like these, there are going to be people who use these ablities for evil, so in response, superheroes began rising up to combat these threats, heroes like yours truly! There are all kinds of super heroes all around the globe, and while its not recognized as an offical occupation, everyone loves and respects them!
